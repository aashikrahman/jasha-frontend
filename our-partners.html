Accreditations and licenses

Jasha Solutions W.L.L is a proud member of BNI - The World's Leading Business Networking and Referral Organization. Within the business circles of Doha, we are recognized as being a very professional, reliable and innovative Creative agency, so chapter members at BNI are happy to refer us time and again. On our part, we ensure referral clients are able to achieve their desired brand objectives after engaging with us.

This has enriched our relationship with clients and created a win-win for both of us. While clients have transformed the way they are perceived in their target market, we have been able to foray into new industry verticals, and prestigious accounts, from time to time. On one hand, this has helped us grow in scale and size. While on the other, we have benefited from new insights, fresh perspectives and interesting experiences, all of which have helped us evolve as professionals and human beings.

Jasha is a full-service brand production agency in Doha, Qatar. It is transforming the brand journey of its clients with value-added insights and a rich spectrum of services.
