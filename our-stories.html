Exceeding Expectations with Pearl Qatar: Installation of more than 180 Signages in and around

Jasha Solutions has recently achieved a remarkable success story through the installation project of 180 signages across Pearl Qatar Island. Initially approached by UDC, we were tasked with revamping approximately 28 signages as part of the island's beautification efforts ahead of the FIFA World Cup. After several rounds of revisions and design submissions, our expertise and dedication earned us the project award.

Our team worked tirelessly to complete the installation work well ahead of the deadline, delivering exceptional results 30 days in advance. This achievement significantly boosted the client's confidence in our capabilities, leading them to entrust us with an additional 100 signages, all to be completed within the same deadline.

Though faced with a daunting challenge, we embraced it as an opportunity to showcase our commitment and expertise. With sheer determination and meticulous planning, we successfully completed the additional signages two days before the deadline. The client expressed their utmost satisfaction and appreciation for our exceptional performance, further solidifying our partnership. They have entrusted us with the remaining signages, removing any specific deadline.

This project has not only elevated our confidence as a team but has also strengthened our long-term relationship with UDC. We are grateful for the opportunity to demonstrate our capabilities and eagerly anticipate working on many more successful projects together.

Mall of Qatar Shines with Stunning and massive Gold Bar Installation for "GOLDLUCK" Promotion

Jasha Solutions recently achieved tremendous success with a unique installation project at the Mall of Qatar as part of their upcoming promotion, "GOLDLUCK." Approached by the mall property management agency, we were tasked with creating a guerilla marketing idea that would captivate customers and embody the promotion's theme. Our team proposed an installation featuring a cluster of oversized golden bars, reminiscent of the real ones, arranged on a platform. The platform also showcased 3D golden letters spelling out the promotion's theme, "GOLD" Crafted meticulously using aluminum and coated with golden paint, the bars were engraved with the Mall of Qatar logo, resembling the real ones.

This gigantic installation left a profound impact on walk-in customers, generating a sense of awe and excitement, while effectively promoting the campaign. Thanks to the success of the installation and the dedication of our team, the promotion achieved remarkable results. We take pride in our team's efforts and their ability to create unforgettable experiences for our clients and their customers.
